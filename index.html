ABOUT // 01

A festival built like a system,
not a schedule.

Techfest is a student-run technology festival running three parallel tracks — Compete, Build, and Learn — under one campus for three days straight. No keynote theatre, no filler: every hour is a working session, a match, or a launch. What started as a single hackathon in a lecture hall is now the country's largest student-led tech gathering, engineered entirely by the people who attend it.
